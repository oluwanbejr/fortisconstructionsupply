Affordable Housing

As complex as the issue of affordable housing is, it can be distilled down to the fact that there are simply not enough low-cost housing units to meet the demand of low-income households. 

With a passion for building what matters for our communities, we take pride in working with developers to bring these critical assets to fruition. Our 100+ years of experience delivering multi-family residences, coupled with our understanding of critical factors, such as tax credit applications and firm construction budgets, enables us to assist clients from feasibility and the earliest stages of project development, all the way to closeout. 

SOME Roberts Residences at 1515 North Capitol

SOME Roberts Residences at 1515 North Capitol is a 14-story, affordable housing facility with 139 units, a second-floor terrace, and amenity space.

SOME is thrilled to have partnered with our friends at Clark Construction on this exciting project. This ribbon-cutting event marks an important milestone for our city and brings us all one step closer to addressing the persistent affordable housing crisis in our region.

Ralph Boyd, president and chief executive officer at SOME

Maria Elena Sifuentes Apartments

Maria Elena Sifuentes Apartments in the Albany Park neighborhood of Chicago is a six-story, residential building with 50 affordable units, and retail space on the ground floor. 

The Exchange at Spring Hill Station

The Exchange at Spring Hill Station is an affordable housing residence that is planned to consist of two, twenty-story residential towers and a two-story community center at the ground level. This development is a substantial milestone in affordable housing for the Tysons, Virginia, area.
