Building What Matters
Alamo Exhibit at the Ralston Family Collections Center

In 2024, Clark delivered the Alamo Exhibit at the Ralston Family Collections Center, a 24,000-square-foot artifact facility with exhibit space, storage rooms, and maintenance workshops.

Orange County Museum of Art

In 2022, Clark delivered the Orange County Museum of Art (OCMA), a 53,000-square-foot art museum. It includes 25,000 square feet of exhibition galleries and an additional 10,000 square feet for education programs, performances, and public gatherings.

Kansas City International Airport (MCI) New Single Terminal

In 2023, Clark delivered the largest infrastructure project in Kansas City, Missouri history with the Kansas City International Airport (MCI) New Single Terminal, transforming how locals and visitors experience travel to and from the Midwest city.

Inova Schar Cancer Institute

The first major project on Inova Health System's 117-acre campus, the 420,000-square-foot Schar Cancer Institute provides complete, patient-centric cancer care and creates a world-class healing environment for patients, families, and the Virginia community.

Music City Center

Located in the heart of downtown Nashville, Music City Center has given the city a bigger stage to host larger events, and, ultimately, has made a significant impact on Nashville's economy.

San Francisco Animal Care & Control Facility

The retrofitted 19th-century unreinforced masonry structure of the San Francisco Animal Care & Control Facility serves as a welcoming environment for the thousands of domestic and wildlife animals that come through its doors every year.

The Wilson & The Elm

Clark worked alongside its talented project partners to deliver The Wilson & the Elm, a monumental mixed-use development in Bethesda, Maryland.
